# Cross-Country Panel Crosswalk v0.1

Canonical constructs:
- Employment
- Education
- Income
- Family
- Health
- Well-being

Comparability notes:
- Employment is approximate because source panels use different labor-force status definitions and reference periods.
- Education is approximate and should be reduced to broad ISCED-style bands.
- Income is approximate because currency, tax basis, household equivalization, and inflation treatment differ.
- Family is approximate because household roster and partnership definitions differ.
- Health is comparable with caution for self-rated health after scale harmonization.
- Well-being is approximate; life satisfaction, depression, and flourishing proxies are not interchangeable.

License boundaries remain panel-specific. Restricted panels remain request-status stubs until access and license status are approved.
