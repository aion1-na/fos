# GSS Public Context

Canonical dataset name: `gss_public_context`

Access status: request-status stub until public aggregate extract terms and codebook version are recorded.

License metadata: source-specific GSS public-use terms must be captured before production ingestion.

Codebook mapping: `codebooks/gss_public_context.yaml` maps public aggregate social participation, trust, and affiliation measures into community-context constructs.

Quality profile: production profiles must document survey year, weights, missingness, sampling error, geography availability, and comparability limits.

Provenance manifest: connector metadata only until an approved public aggregate extract is staged with content hash and source URI.

Access policy: public aggregate tables only. Restricted or individual-level extracts require separate governed access and cannot be substituted with synthetic rows.

Limitations: survey aggregates describe population context, not individual behavior.

Inappropriate uses: do not prescribe religious, civic, or community participation. Treat measures as voluntary social pathways for contextual validation only.
