RS-06 - Expand the intervention library and attach auditable effect-size priors to each target-trial scenario.

Objective: Expand the intervention library and attach auditable effect-size priors to each target-trial scenario.

Implement deliverables:
- EvidenceClaim schema upgrades: target population, treatment, comparator, outcome domain, effect size, uncertainty, risk-of-bias, transportability, citation, and source dataset_reference
- Curated claims for job training/retraining, apprenticeships, mentoring, financial stabilization/cash transfers, mental-health access, community belonging/social prescribing, education support, healthcare decoupling, EITC, MTO, Job Corps, and Project STAR
- features.intervention_effect_size_priors_v1
- Curator review workflow and status labels: draft, advisor_reviewed, rejected, superseded
- Atlas forest-plot view for intervention priors

Acceptance gates:
- At least 75 draft evidence claims and at least 25 advisor-ready claims exist
- Every effect size has source, estimate, uncertainty, population, treatment, comparator, and outcome domain
- By-request datasets are represented as request-status records, not fake microdata
- Intervention priors can be queried by transition model and research brief generator

Constraints:
- Do not fabricate data. Fixtures are allowed only for unit tests and must be named fixture_only.
- Every production-facing dataset must have a dataset card, license/access metadata, codebook mapping, quality profile, provenance manifest, and content hash.
- Every simulation-facing output must use dataset_reference = (canonical_dataset_name, version, content_hash).
- MiroFish outputs are interaction/narrative artifacts unless explicitly validated; they must not create causal effect sizes.
- Keep changes scoped to this sprint and do not commit automatically.
