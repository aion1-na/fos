RS-06 - Add health, community, time-use, and place-context pathways.

Objective: Ingest public health, social-capital, community, time-use, and place-context data to parameterize non-work pathways in the flagship simulation.

Implement deliverables:
- Connectors for CDC/BRFSS/NHIS/NHANES/MEPS public components where available and legal
- Connectors for GSS, ATUS, Pew Religious Landscape Study, Volunteering and Civic Life, Social Capital Atlas, Opportunity Atlas, OECD/World Bank context where needed
- features.health_validation_context, features.community_context, features.time_use_context, and features.place_context
- Small-cell suppression and sensitive-variable policy checks
- Dataset cards that distinguish individual microdata, public aggregates, and request-status records

Acceptance gates:
- Community and place features join only where legally and methodologically valid
- Unsupported or restricted data receives request-status records, not fake tables
- Feature tables can be linked to FOS population agents through documented geography/segment joins
- Health and community pathways are marked as calibration or validation inputs, not causal proof by themselves

Constraints:
- Do not fabricate data. Fixtures are allowed only for unit tests and must be named fixture_only.
- Every production-facing dataset must have a dataset card, license/access metadata, codebook mapping, quality profile, provenance manifest, and content hash.
- Every simulation-facing output must use dataset_reference = (canonical_dataset_name, version, content_hash).
- Concordia outputs are qualitative interaction/cognition artifacts unless explicitly validated; Concordia must not create causal effect sizes.
- FOS Graph outputs are visual/analytic artifacts; graph layout or animation must not be treated as evidence.
- Keep changes scoped to this sprint and do not commit automatically.
